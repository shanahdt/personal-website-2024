Daniel Shanahan is an associate professor of Music Theory and Cognition at Northwestern University, 
where he co-directs the (https://sites.northwestern.edu/mccll/)[Music Cognition Lab]. 
He has more than fifty publications, and is the editor 
of the forthcoming _Oxford Handbook of Music and Corpus Studies_, and is currently working on a monograph 
tentatively titled _Networks of Musical Knowledge_.
